The NEET eligibility criteria include the following:

Minimum age: Candidates must have completed 17 years as of December 31 of the year of his/her admission.

Maximum age limit: There is no upper age limit for the exam.

Citizens: Indian citizens, Overseas Citizens of India (OCI), and foreign nationals are eligible to appear in the exam.

Candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English as core subjects from a recognized board.

The class 12 percentage required for NEET 2024 exam is separate for each category and can be listed as under. NEET eligibility marks in 12th to be considered are aggregate marks only for PCB subjects

For General Category  50% (Physically Handicapped, PH  45%) 
OBC/SC/ST  40% 
PWD  45%
Note: The marks scored for Mathematics would not be taken into consideration in this exam.
