NEET: 

Full Form: National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Undergraduate)

The National Eligibility Entrance Test (NEET) is one of the major entrance tests conducted in India held by the National Testing Agency (NTA) 

NEET (UG) The National Eligibility Entrance Test (Undergraduate), previously known as the All India Pre Medical Test (AIPMT) NEET started in 2013.

There are three types of NEET examinations: NEET UG, NEET PG, and NEET SS. So, class 11 and 12 graders are preparing for the NEET UG exam that they will appear for after clearing board examinations

Students who have passed the class XII qualifying exam are eligible to apply for NEET. NEET stands for National Eligibility cum Entrance Test. It is a single level national exam carried out for medical aspirants enabling them to seek admissions to medical courses across different medical institutes of the country and also, for those intending to pursue primary medical qualification abroad.

It will be conducted once in a year
